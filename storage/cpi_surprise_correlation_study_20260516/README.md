# CPI surprise correlation study

Contract: `cpi_surprise_correlation_study_v1`

This study evaluates CPI actual-vs-forecast surprises from Investing.com public event pages against local ETF bars and nearby controls. Large surprise definitions are absolute actual-minus-forecast gaps of at least 0.1pp and 0.2pp across headline/core MoM/YoY CPI.

Conclusion: CPI surprise is more useful than raw CPI level as a macro event-risk feature, but still not enough for standalone directional alpha.
